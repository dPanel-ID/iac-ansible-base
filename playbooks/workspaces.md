# Workspace Playbooks

Free-tier dPanel workspaces share this repository. Generated workspace
playbooks are stored under:

```text
playbooks/team-<WORKSPACE-ID>/
playbooks/personal-<USER-ID>/
```

These folders are created by dPanel on demand. Keep common playbooks such as
`setup.yml`, `build.yml`, `deploy.yml`, `pipeline.yml`, and `router.yml` in the
top-level `playbooks/` folder.
